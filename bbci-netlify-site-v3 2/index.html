How it works

  1. Identify the right acres

    We focus on opportunity acres where corridors, strips, buffers, or perennial cover reduce headaches and add value.

  2. Build a practical plan

    We develop a field-ready proposal: acres, practices, monitoring approach, and a payment pathway.

  3. Monitor outcomes

    We handle monitoring and reporting so payments can reflect verified results—without adding burden to your operation.

  4. Receive payments

    Annual payments reflect outcomes like improved habitat, reduced runoff, and managed perennial cover.
